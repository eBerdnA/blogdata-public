From my point view anyone working in a field where one needs to keep their knowledge up to date needs to embrace lifelong learning. [Upgrade your learning + an example study plan for data structures and algorithms](https://dev.to/codebalance/upgrade-your-learning-an-example-study-plan-for-data-structures-and-algorithms-5he) shows a great strategy how you can improve your learning game. \#AlwaysBeLearning

Azure Functions got some new features. [Jeremy Likness](https://twitter.com/jeremylikness/) shows how you can facilitate all these features in his blog post [Serverless HTTP With Durable Functions](https://blog.jeremylikness.com/blog/serverless-http-with-durable-functions/).

Very interesting read on the top 10 areas of app development which are being reshaped by Power Apps. [Power Apps Top 10: innovation areas that are reshaping app development](https://powerapps.microsoft.com/en-us/blog/power-apps-top-10-innovation-areas-that-are-reshaping-app-development/).
